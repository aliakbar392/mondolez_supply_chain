A Transparent Supply Chain

1. Ethical Sourcing

Idukki Hills, Kerala, India

Certified Fair Trade & Organic

Grown using agroforestry methods to promote biodiversity. We pay a 15% premium above market price directly to our farming partners.

2. Natural Processing

At the Cooperative

Zero-Emission & Water-Wise

Beans are fermented to develop flavor, then sun-dried on raised beds—a zero-emission process powered by nature. Water used is recycled for local irrigation.

3. Green Crafting

Our Atelier in Mumbai

Zero-Waste & Ethical Labor

Our chocolatiers roast and conch beans in our green-certified atelier. We ensure fair wages for our team and 98% of production waste is recycled or composted.
