A Transparent Journey

1. Ethical Sourcing

Idukki Hills, Kerala, India

Our journey begins with single-origin cacao beans, ethically harvested by the farming cooperatives in the lush, rain-fed hills of Idukki. We partner directly with farmers, ensuring fair wages and supporting sustainable agricultural practices.

2. Fermentation & Drying

At the Cooperative

The harvested beans are fermented for 5-7 days in wooden boxes to develop their rich, complex flavor profile. Afterwards, they are sun-dried on raised beds, a traditional method that preserves their quality and aroma.

3. Crafting the Chocolate

Our Atelier in Mumbai

The dried beans arrive at our Mumbai atelier. Here, our chocolatiers expertly roast, winnow, conch, and temper the beans in small batches. This meticulous process transforms them into the smooth, decadent chocolate you love.
