Our Traceability & Impact Commitment

Farm-to-Point Traceability

We insist on documented traceability from the farm to the first purchase point, conducting regular spot audits to ensure accuracy.

Deforestation Monitoring

Every farm in our program is digitally mapped, allowing us to actively monitor and prevent any cases of deforestation.

Child Labor Monitoring (CLMRS)

We implement a robust Child Labor Monitoring and Remediation System (CLMRS) across all communities we partner with.

Continuous Impact Assessment

We constantly measure our social and environmental impact, using the data to learn, adapt, and improve our programs.

Third-Party Verification

To ensure complete impartiality, our process is verified by world-class independent auditors.
